Help & Usage Instructions

This tool provides multiple encryption and decryption methods to secure your text, including Basic Encryption, Base64 encoding/decoding, and a unique DexterEnc emoji-based cipher. Additionally, it features a Random Password Generator to create strong passwords tailored to your preferences.

  • Choose the desired function by selecting a tab from the sidebar.
  • Input your text in the provided area and click the corresponding button to perform encryption or decryption.
  • Results will be displayed in the output area below the input field, which is read-only for your convenience.
  • Use the "Copy" button to easily copy any generated output or password to your clipboard.
